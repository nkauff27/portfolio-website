My Work

Project

Peapod Pediatrics

Summary

Create a mobile application that connects parents or guardians of children to a pediatrician 24/7 in order to diagnose and treat specific symptoms and illnesses

Duration

7 Months

Project Goal

The goal is to determine if users can easily and properly navigate the application and its various tools and features. The primary users would be parents who may be stressed or hurried to find an answer for their child’s illness(s) and navigating a streamlined, easy to use journey for them to reach their goals will be paramount.

Understand the User

To create an effective pediatric expert app, I searched for parents of young children who may have a hectic lifestyle, are concerned about today's environment, and/or are looking for an alternative pathway to reach a doctor with the ease of their phone.

Site Map

Before jumping into the wireframing for this application, I worked on the information architecture to lay out a proper sitemap. From the study’s findings, there was a better understanding of how users categorize concepts and if changes were needed to the current IA. I was then able to revise the sitemap for a more streamlined experience.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

By using my user stories, flows, and site map, I was able to create a mid-fidelity wireframe for the basic layout of the app.

High-Fidelity Wireframes

After user feedback and iterations, I was able to create high-fidelity layouts of the app.
